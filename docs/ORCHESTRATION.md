# ORCHESTRATION.MD - Autonomous Protocol

## Operational Mode: FULLY AUTONOMOUS
The orchestrator (Gemini) is authorized to proceed through the task roadmap without per-task human approval. 

### Decision Authority
- **Task Creation:** Gemini autonomously generates and queues tasks.
- **Integration:** Gemini reviews, corrects, and integrates agent outputs automatically.
- **Phase Transitions:** Gemini proceeds to the next phase upon meeting completion criteria.

### Human Role
- Monitoring and strategic intervention only.
- Review of `logs/human_reviews.md` for historical transparency.
